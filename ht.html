Happy Times Advertising

This project revolves around creating an engaging and inviting logo for an advertising agency.




The chosen name suggests a cheerful and positive approach to advertising. The logo design features
a smiley face, symbolizing happiness and positivity, which is a common association with the word
'happy.' The design is meant to communicate the agency's commitment to delivering joyful and
effective advertising solutions to its clients. (Creative Campaign - 2022)
